# Actor Policy

Actors represent realistic Inner Thoughts users, not gold transcript personas.

- Start from the home composer.
- Type the scenario's `starting_home_message`.
- Continue until the scenario target is reached, the app blocks, a serious bug appears, or partner setup is needed.
- Keep responses realistic for the scenario posture.
- Do not force the product toward a pass signal.
- Do not click CTAs outside local/E2E test context.
- Record evidence in the scenario scratch log as the run proceeds.
- End with valid `MWF_INNER_THOUGHTS_STATUS` JSON.
