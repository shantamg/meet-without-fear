# MWF Prompt Proposal v06

Scenario: `adam-eve`
Source run: `eval/runs/20260505-200823-adam-eve-iter-02`
Focus: Keep Stage 2 share suggestions at an earned gate and clean up facilitator copy.

## Evidence

The run stayed at `overall_score: 3.0` and `mwf_handling: 3`. Actor fidelity stayed strong, but Stage 2 still did not reach a clean boundary:

- `transcripts/eve-stage2.md`: Eve received a share suggestion immediately after her first Stage 2 answer, before she had completed her empathy statement.
- `transcripts/adam-stage2.md`: Adam later received a visible share suggestion after Eve shared context, but the exchange stopped before Adam responded.
- `scratch/2026-05-05-cmoth9jcr00abpx8nv3ak34pb-eve.md`: Eve stayed blocked on Adam's unresolved share offer across repeated continuation checks.
- `transcripts/eve-stage2.md`: facilitator copy included `hes not enough`, a small but visible polish failure.

## Proposed Prompt Change

In `backend/src/services/stage-prompts.ts`, keep the v05 Stage 2 timing guard and add a copy-quality guard:

```text
Optional context sharing belongs at an earned between-step gate. Do not surface a share suggestion immediately after the user's first empathy answer unless the user is explicitly unable to continue without naming their own experience. First help them complete a coherent empathy attempt.

After a partner shares context, the next facilitator message must name the concrete continuation: read what was shared, say what changed in your understanding, then revise or confirm the empathy statement. Do not leave the next action implicit.

Before returning user-visible copy, scan contractions and possessives for plain-language correctness. Preserve apostrophes in phrases such as "he's", "she's", "that's", "you're", and "partner's".
```

## Product Guard To Pair With This Prompt

If a share suggestion is created for a user during Stage 2, the session state and UI must expose it as the active user's next action until they accept, refine, or decline it. A partner should not remain in repeated `needs_partner` waits on a share offer that the active user has no visible path to answer.

## Regression Tests

- Add a Stage 2 flow test where both empathy attempts exist, one share offer is `OFFERED`, and the subject's session summary/UI state reports a self action.
- Add a focused copy-cleanliness assertion for Stage 2 generated facilitator text or transcript extraction so obvious contraction loss is caught.
- Re-run the Adam/Eve gold loop through Stage 2 and confirm there are no unresolved `OFFERED` share offers at the stop boundary.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Stage 2 optional context sharing does not interrupt the first empathy attempt.
- A visible share suggestion is resolved before either side reports Stage 2 complete.
- `mwf_handling` improves from `3` toward `4`.
- `actor_fidelity` remains at `4`.
