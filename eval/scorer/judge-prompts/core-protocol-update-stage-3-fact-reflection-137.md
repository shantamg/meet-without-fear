### The Needs Share
Both needs summaries are held until both users complete and confirm Stage 3. Then each user is asked for consent to share their summary with their partner, following the same pattern as the empathy consent in Stage 2: the AI names exactly what it would share, and the user explicitly approves before anything is sent.
