# Dispatch: FEEDBACK

**Goal:** Acknowledge and track for the team.

## Steps

1. **Create issue** per `shared/github/create-issue.md`:
   - Label: `feedback`
   - Title: summarize the feedback (<80 chars)
   - Body: exact feedback, who gave it, provenance block
   - Attach images if present

2. **Reply in thread** acknowledging the feedback warmly. See `references/response-templates.md`.
