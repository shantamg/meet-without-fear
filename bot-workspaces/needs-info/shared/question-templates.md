# Question Templates

Clarifying questions organized by category. Pick 2-3 per round. Adapt wording to match what the user has already shared — never ask for info they already provided.

## Bug Reports

### Round 1 (Initial)
- Which screen or feature were you using when this happened?
- What did you expect to happen vs what actually happened?
- Can you describe the steps you took right before the issue occurred?

### Round 2 (Follow-up)
- Does this happen every time, or only sometimes?
- Did you see any error messages or unexpected behavior on screen?
- What device/browser are you using?

### Round 3 (Final details)
- When did you first notice this issue? (approximate date/time)
- Were there any recent changes to your account or setup?

## Feature Requests

### Round 1 (Initial)
- What problem are you trying to solve? (describe the need, not the solution)
- Who would use this feature? (just you, your family, all users?)
- How are you currently working around this?

### Round 2 (Follow-up)
- Can you describe what the ideal experience would look like?
- How often do you run into this need?
- Is there an existing feature that partially addresses this?

## Questions / Confusion

### Round 1 (Initial)
- Which part of the app are you asking about?
- What were you trying to do when you got confused?
- Have you tried any of the options available on that screen?

### Round 2 (Follow-up)
- Is there a specific term or label that's unclear?
- Would a walkthrough of that feature be helpful?
